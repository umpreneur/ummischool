🔹 1. Blog Page Title and Intro

Keep the page title as “Blog”.

Subheading underneath: Over dagen, groei en alles daartussen.

Layout: clean text, centered, serif font (Cormorant Garamond), light gray subtitle in #6B655E.

No line or box — just white space above and below.

🔹 2. Main Section – Featured Posts

Display 2–3 featured blog posts at the top of the page.

Each post should include:

A horizontal image (neutral tones, no bright colors).

Post title (in serif font, black #1E1E1E).

Short 2–3 line intro.

“Lees verder →” link in accent color #A5672B.

Maintain generous spacing between featured posts.

On hover, image slightly fades or lifts (minimal effect, no box shadow).

🔹 3. Side Section – “Meer lezen”

On desktop: right column listing other blog titles (no images).

On mobile: list appears below featured posts.

Section title: “Meer lezen” (or “Andere stukken”).

Each title functions as a link — simple black text, date optional in warm gray #6B655E.

Add subtle line above this section (1px, warm gray).

No icons, no frames, just text and space.

🔹 4. Layout & Responsiveness

Desktop: two-column layout

Left: featured posts

Right: list of titles (“Meer lezen”)

Mobile: one-column layout

Featured posts first

Title list below

Maintain large margins and white space throughout (no grid borders).

🔹 5. Colors & Typography
Element	Color	HEX
Text	Soft black	#1E1E1E
Accent (links)	Warm brown-orange	#A5672B
Hover background	Light beige	#EDE7E0
Background	Off-white	#FAF9F7
Subtext / date	Warm gray	#6B655E

Fonts:

Titles → Cormorant Garamond (serif, weight 500–600)

Body text → Inter or Manrope (sans-serif, weight 300–400)

Line spacing: 1.6–1.8

🔹 6. Content Style

No category buttons or filters (keep page minimal).

Limit the total number of images to 3–4 on the whole page.

Focus on calm rhythm, white space, and typography.

All buttons and “Lees verder” links use accent color #A5672B.

🔹 7. Blog Post Pages

When opening a single post:

Large serif title on top.

Date in small warm gray text.

Wide margins, no sidebar.

Light background (#FAF9F7), no borders or frames.

That’s all for this update — the goal is to make the Blog page feel calm, editorial, and balanced,
with a few visual highlights at the top and a clean text-based list for the rest.
